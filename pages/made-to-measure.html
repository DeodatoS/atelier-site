The Process

First step - Book an appointment

Whether you are in Milano or overseas, schedule your appointment with our specialists. Bookings can be made through telephone, online, or in person at our atelier.

Second step - Measurements

We take precise measurements for each individual client through an extensive process with attention to detail. Aesthetic aspects such as silhouette, design and style are thoroughly discussed.

Approximately 45 minutes

Third step - Fitting

The second meeting is scheduled 4 weeks after the first. During this meeting a primary fitting is conducted on the garment, alterations are made and the fit is refined.

Approximately 45 minutes

Fourth step - Collection

The piece is ready to be collected in store, perfectly tailored to your measurements and preferences.
