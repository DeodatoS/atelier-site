Our Atelier

Faithful to the Italian origins of the brand, our Atelier and team pays tribute to craftsmanship

In fashion, we embrace slowness—choosing quality over quantity, and timeless design over fleeting trends. Sustainability means crafting pieces with care, sourcing responsibly, and honoring the planet with every stitch. We believe that less is not just more—it's better.

Atelier

We believe in building garments through deep research—with those who share our values, our heritage, and our future. Understanding how our manufacturers work is not just important and essential to us. Together, we craft each piece with intention, blending tradition and innovation like a true family.

We are proud to partner with master artisans who embody the spirit of Italian excellence. Their expertise, rooted in generations of craftsmanship, brings our vision to life with every stitch and detail. From our signature 70's style design every element of our process reflects a shared reverence for artistry and authenticity.

Elisa Sanna is more than a brand. It's a family bound by respect, creativity, and a commitment to timeless Italian craftsmanship.
