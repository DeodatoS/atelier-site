Hand Embroidery

The art of transforming fabric into expressive artwork through needle and thread

Hand embroidery is where patience meets artistry. Unlike machine embroidery, every stitch is placed with intention, creating pieces that carry the soul of the artisan. From delicate floral motifs to bold geometric designs, our hand embroidery transforms simple cloth into wearable masterpieces.

Artisanal Excellence

Each thread is carefully selected for its quality and color, ensuring that every embroidered piece meets our standards of excellence. Our artisans spend years perfecting their craft, mastering traditional Italian techniques passed down through generations.

The process begins with a detailed sketch, followed by precise pattern placement on the fabric. Every stitch is placed by hand, creating textures and depths that machine embroidery simply cannot replicate. The result is a piece that tells a unique story through its craftsmanship.

Premium Threads

Silk and cotton threads selected for their lustrous quality and color fastness, ensuring longevity and beauty.

Traditional Techniques

Time-honored methods including satin stitch, French knots, and chain stitch, each creating unique textures and effects.

Intricate Patterns

From delicate florals to contemporary geometric designs, each pattern is carefully planned and executed by master artisans.

Italian Heritage

Our hand embroidery tradition is rooted in the rich textile heritage of Italy, where embroidery has been perfected over centuries. We honor this legacy while incorporating contemporary design elements that speak to modern sensibilities.

Each piece takes between 20 to 100 hours to complete, depending on the complexity of the design. This investment of time and skill is what makes every Elisa Sanna embroidered piece truly special and irreplaceable.
