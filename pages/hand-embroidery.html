Hand Embroidery

Hand embroidery is the art of decorating fabric using a needle and thread. Unlike machine embroidery, it's entirely done by hand allowing for a personal touch, intricate details, and endless creative possibilities. Whether you're stitching a delicate floral motif or a bold geometric design, hand embroidery transforms simple cloth into expressive artwork.

At Elisa Sanna, each piece is meticulously hand-embroidered by our skilled artisans, preserving traditional Italian craftsmanship while embracing contemporary design. Every stitch tells a story of dedication, precision, and artistic excellence.

Our hand embroidery process involves careful selection of premium threads, precise pattern placement, and hours of skilled needlework to create unique pieces that celebrate the beauty of slow fashion and artisanal mastery.
