PERSONALISED FOR YOU

Made to Measure is not intended to replace custom tailoring for you. Collaborate with our artisans to choose the finest fabrics and styles every detail. Each design is tailored to your personal style, bringing Italian craftsmanship with modern sophistication.

Precision

A beautifully tailored custom garment is created from a thoughtful design process, perfectly crafted to the client's measurements and personal preferences.

Fit and Design

Made to measure starts with an existing base pattern that is modified to fit the client. Options like fabric, style and other design elements are available, but the structure of the garment remains largely pre-determined.

Exclusivity

Made to measure provides a personal fit and variation in fabrics, options, offering a unique garment designed with your needs in mind.

THE PROCESS

First step: Appointment

Schedule them to Mauro's specialist by appointment only. Please note that fittings can be made without immediate attention of placing an order.

Second step: Measurement & Consultation

The unique silhouette designed for each individual client is created through an advanced measurement process with an extensive attention to detail. A specific silhouette such as sleeve, design and style are thoroughly discussed. The client is closely guided by the experienced through the process.

Third step: Fitting

The garment is scheduled if needed for the first meeting. During the second meeting a thorough fitting is conducted on the garment ordered. Alterations are made and the fit is perfected to the simple future without compromising through the process.

Finishing: Collection

The piece is ready to be collected in store.
